Intellectual to intellectual: Stop watching endless documentaries. Stop dissecting podcasts. Stop scrolling through think pieces and “10 ways to fix your mind” threads. Start reading fiction. Start watching cartoons and comfort shows. Go outside without headphones. Let your brain breathe. Constant self-improvement isn’t evolution, it’s brain rot disguised as productivity. You don’t need to study every emotion to heal it. Not everything that hurts needs to be analyzed. Some things just need to be lived through. You don’t need to turn every feeling into a theory. Sometimes peace is found in doing something that doesn’t need to make sense. You’re not here just to understand life. You’re here to feel it.
